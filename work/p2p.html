Peer to Peer Payments

Feature to pay a person based on phone number

What it is

Design a feature for a user of our payments app to quickly and easily transfer money to his/her friends


User Stories

Based on user interviews, the following user stories were prepared

Story 1

Uday is a user of this app. He owes his friend Sam, who is also on the app, Rs.300 for lunch. He doesn’t have cash right now and neither does he want to go through the hassle of asking for Sam’s bank details, adding him as a beneficiary, and waiting for 24 hours for the request to process.

As Uday: I would like to pay Sam instantly and easily.

Story 2

Sofie is a user of this app. She meets her friend Kartik (not a user) for a drink and Kartik pays the bill and says, “Let’s settle it later”. Sofie wants to pay Kartik through her balance on the app but isn’t sure if he is a user already.

As Sofie: I would like to pay Kartik irrespective of whether Kartik is already a user of the app or not. Also, I would like to be able to split bills with friends easily.

Story 3

Aji and Manoj both use the app. Aji owes Manoj some money. Manoj urgently needs to make a few payments and wants to remind Aji to return his money and get it soon.

As Manoj: I would like to request Aji for the money along with a brief message. Would also like to able to receive the money instantly once Aji approves the request.
As Aji: I would like to be able to immediately approve Manoj’s request and return his money instantly.


Features from tasks

  1. Send money to other people using their mobile numbers as the search key. People can be searched in the contact list or found by entering their number.
  2. Send money to any mobile number irrespective of whether the person is currently a user of the app. If not currently a user, the person gets an SMS notification and shall receive the amount when he/she installs the app.
  3. Instant transfer
  4. Split bills (Deferred for a future release)
  5. Request money (Deferred for a future release)
