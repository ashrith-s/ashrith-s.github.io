What Makes a Song Likeable?

Analyzing Spotify’s Top Tracks Of 2017 Using Data Visualization



Project details

This is the final project for the course INST760: Data Visualization


Motivation

Spotify is one of the leading music streaming apps of today with more than 83 million paid subscribers as of June 2018. Spotify compiles a yearly list of Top Songs based on the number of times the songs were streamed by users. It is never easy to tell which song might get popular and which song might not, irrespective of the quality of it or the talent involved to make it. As a musician, this has always intrigued me and hence I have always been curious about what really makes a song likable to the masses.

For this Data Visualization project, I wish to analyze the songs that were featured on Spotify’s Top Tracks of 2017 using data visualization techniques to figure out patterns that would help us identify what makes a song likable. I will do this by comparing the characteristics of the songs broadly classified by Spotify as Audio Features.
