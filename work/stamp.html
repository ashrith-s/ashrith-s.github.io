Simplify 'Stamp' Event Discovery

Understanding Roadblocks Faced by UMD Students



Project Details

This is the final project for the course INST710: User Experience Research Methods


Motivation

The Adele H. Stamp Student Union, commonly referred to as "Stamp", is the student activity center on the campus of the University of Maryland, College Park. Each year, more than 3000 events happen in or around Stamp.

We will conduct user research using Contextual Design to understand how students discover and track events. By employing methods like Contextual Interviews, Affinity Mapping, and using Sequence and Day-In-The-Life Models, we hope to elicit insights into students' behaviors, and propose the best ways for the marketing team to engage with students
