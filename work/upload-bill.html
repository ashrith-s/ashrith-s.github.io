Universal Acceptance


Feature to allow spending meal vouchers at all food merchants



Design Brief

This app is used by corporate employees to pay for food items using their employer-issued, tax-deductible meal vouchers. Currently users can easily make payments to merchants who are on our payment network. But our users want to use their vouchers at other food outlets as well. Our Sales team is working on getting these other merchants on board but this is a time-consuming process. Right now these users have no option but to pay by cash. Also, a lot of prospective clients are hesitant to come on board because our merchant network is still growing and not as sizeable as our competitors. Hence we need to design a feature where we can let users who are paying by cash to upload their bills and get the cash reimbursed in return for meal vouchers. With this our app can claim universal acceptance from any food merchant in India.


Product Requirements

  1. Our merchant list should now include all merchants both on and off our payment network. Merchants who aren't on the network yet are denoted as "Pay and Claim".
  2. On choosing a merchant we show an informational screen stating how the process works. This is because it is a new feature and some amount of educating the user is required. We can configure this screen so that it only appears the first few times he tries to pay a non-network merchant
  3. Launch camera to click a photo of the bill
  4. Enter claim amount. This is so that user can choose to claim only a portion of the bill, in case the bill has non-food items as well.
  5. After submitting, the claim goes to our Operations team where our personnel scan the bill and verify that is a genuine bill and that the claim amount only includes food items. (This is to maintain compliance with Government regulations). Operations personnel can then either approve or reject the claim.
  6. User gets a notification about approval/rejection. Upon approval he can click to claim the amount. Approved amount is deducted from his meal voucher balance and added to his cash balance on the app.
